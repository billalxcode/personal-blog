## Welcome

Welcome to my blog! This is the first article on this platform, and I'm excited to share my thoughts, ideas, and learnings with you.

## What This Blog Is About

This blog covers topics I'm passionate about:

- **Machine Learning** — From foundational concepts to cutting-edge research
- **Agentic AI** — Building autonomous systems that can reason and act
- **Computer Vision** — Understanding and processing visual information
- **Software Engineering** — Best practices, architecture, and tooling

## Why I Started Writing

I believe that writing is one of the best ways to solidify understanding. When you can explain something clearly, you truly understand it. This blog serves as both a knowledge base for myself and a resource for others who are on a similar learning journey.

## What's Different Here

Unlike my research journals (which follow a formal LaTeX format), articles on this blog are more casual and accessible. Think of them as:

> Conversations about technology, wrapped in code and curiosity.
